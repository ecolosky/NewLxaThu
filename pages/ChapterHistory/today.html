The Chapter Today

The active chapter is strong once again. The active membership stands at 30 (December 2002). The chapter house is still standing, although work is needed--as it always has been at 36 Sidney Place. For several years, Lambda Chi Alpha was the only fraternity on campus. But several new fraternities have formed on campus: Tau Delta Phi, Nu Alpha Phi, and Omega Phi Alpha. And Alpha Phi Delta--the Italian-American fraternity that was at Poly in the '60s--has been recolonized. This past year, Poly appointed someone to be advisor to the Greek system; I believe that's a first at Poly.

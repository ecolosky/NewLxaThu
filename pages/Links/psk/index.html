Defining, Calculating, and Representing psk Values

Don Hayes and Roger Grice were reminiscing about Pete Kelly--things we recalled about what he had said, done, and was. Don suggested that Pete should be imortalized by having a mathematical constant named after him--the psk. We agreed that it would have to be a newly developed constant--most likely a variable, multi-dimensional constant that could be used to rate how closely a person, a specific action, or a group of action reflected Pete's spirit.

We started developing the constant, and present it here for you perusal. It is truly a work in progress--and probably always will be so. It is suitable fodder for late-night discussions at reunions, gatherings, and other suitable events.

Please feel free to add to, delete from, change, or amplify what is there. This should truly be a group fraternal effort.

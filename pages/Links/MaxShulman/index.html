Max Shulman's columns from The Poly

Seems hard to believe with all the bans on smoking these days, but cigarettes, especially Marlboros, were a big part of college life in the '60s. Marlboro reps were frequently on campus giving out sample packs; Marlboro sponsored an annual competition to see which campus group could collect and hand in the most empty Marlboro (and Phillip Morris) packs--that's how we won the house hi-fi; and they sponsored a weekly column in The Poly, the school newspaper.

Here is a collection of some of the columns. Enjoy!
